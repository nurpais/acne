Acne Treatment

This skin treatment is suitable for skin problems such as: acne, adult acne, teenage acne

Watch Video

Acne treatment

60 minutes

The acne specialist's treatment for acne is suitable for you regardless of age who have problems with acne (pimples). The treatment is effective for those who want to remove acne / pimples on the face, chest or back. During acne treatment, the following is done:

First, we cleanse the skin with a peel wash that comes down to the deep and dissolves the plugs and removes dead skin cells. After that, we steam the skin with a vapo zone and then do a thorough port evaluation, in some cases even a small lancet is used to open the pores. Then the face is cleansed with a bactericidal face water and then applied to a specially adapted clay mask that suppresses redness and tightens the pores. The treatment ends with a very moisturizing day cream.
