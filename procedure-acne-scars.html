Treatment Acne Scars

This skin procedure is suitable for skin problems such as: acne scars

Microdermabrasion and Microneedling

AcneSpecialist's treatments for acne scars are developed to treat scarring mainly on the face and back but also other parts of the body where you have had acne. Acne scars come in many different forms, which is why we offer both microdermabrasion and microneedling to give you a treatment that suits your scars.

NOTE! You must always carry out a consultation before procedure!

During the consultation we go through your skin and your acne scars to find out which treatment is right for your skin. We also develop a treatment plan and look at how many treatments will be required for a really good result. Of course we also answer all your questions.

Microdermabrasion

30 minutes

Microdermabrasion effectively treats milder acne scars by grinding and moisturizing the skin. A nozzle sucks up the skin with a vacuum, which is then ground using laser-cut diamond crystals. The number of treatments depends on the amount of scarring on the skin, but we usually recommend 5-10 treatments to achieve the best results.

Microdermabrasion can also be used to treat: pigment changes, scarring, sensitive skin, sun-damaged and aging skin, dryness and also reduces pores.

Microneedling

50 minutes

Microneedling is an effective treatment for more severe acne scars on the face and back. The treatment uses a pen-like apparatus that contains microneedles that create a series of smaller vertical penetrating holes in the skin. Microneedling is clinically proven to dramatically reduce most types of skin imperfections such as acne scars, surgical scars, fine lines, wrinkles, enlarged pores, and stretch marks. The treatment makes the skin softer and denser which makes it look more youthful. During the treatment we also apply hyaluronic acid active agents which are absorbed deep into the skin.

Benefits of Microneedling
  • Suitable for all skin types
  • Tanned skin can also be treated
  • Also suitable for thin and sensitive skin

In order for you to get the best possible results, a consultation is conducted where we create a treatment plan to get you the best possible results. Here we go over what you should think about and of course we answer all your questions. For best results, 4-6 treatments are usually recommended at 3-week intervals depending on the type of skin and the amount of acne you have.
