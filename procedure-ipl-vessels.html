IPL Blood Vessels

This skin procedure is suitable for skin problems such as: superficial blood vessels, broken blood vessels

Treat Superficial Blood Vessels

Superficial blood vessels and broken blood vessels can be effectively treated with IPL. IPL stands for "Intense Pulse Light" and consists of a powerful pulse of light combined with a special radio frequency that heats the hemoglobin's dye. When the hemoglobin is heated, the vessel's walls collapse and the blood vessel disappears. This method removes smaller vessels immediately, while for larger vessels it may take a few weeks before the skin's immune system completely breaks down the vessel and seals it away. In some cases, multiple treatments may be needed every few weeks. At AcneSpecialisten we can treat all types of superficial and broken blood vessels and this includes rosacea.

NOTE! You must always carry out a consultation before procedure!

During the consultation, we will go through your skin and your superficial blood vessels to find out how many treatments will be needed.
