Information about Oily Skin

The acne specialist deals with all types of acne, also called acne vulgaris. Acne often occurs in your teens when you produce more sex hormones called testosterone. These hormones easily get into imbalance which in turn stimulates sebum production in the hair follicles, it becomes an overproduction of sebum.

This aggressive fatty acid, in turn, starts to irritate and damage the skin, the skin then wants to remove this and counteract this by creating an inflammation of the skin, the skin and the body's defense against bacteria, that is now you see a pimple. In some cases, only an open blackhead is formed, which allows the sebum to leak. In other cases, the hair follicle may burst inward and acne scars may occur.

This overproduction makes it easily crowded or stops in the mouth of the hair follicle. The sebum then begins to accumulate in the hair follicle, which causes it to soon heal and the bacteria find here, the (propionibacterium acnes) bacterium. These bacteria convert the good fat, which should lubricate and protect the skin to a more aggressive fatty acid.

Types of Acne

To make it easy, one can divide acne into two categories, teenage acne and adult acne. When in life one gets acne varies and in some cases, the acne can change into adult acne. It is therefore important to understand the different types of acne and why it is important to find a long-term solution to the acne.

Teenage acne is what you usually think of when talking about acne. The problem is that in the teenage years there is an increased production of the sex hormone testosterone which in turn leads to the overproduction of sebum in the hair follicles. So it is not because the skin is unclean and if you try to wash the skin excessively with soap and water, the skin is dried out and becomes dry and irritated. By using the right skin care products, the skin's natural balance is restored which effectively keeps the acne under control.

Acne in adults is common and can range from getting occasional pimples to having a constant problem. The reason for adult acne is difficult to assess but can occur during stress, sunlight, seasons, climate, cosmetics, pregnancy, menstruation and more.

Acne Treatment

Unfortunately, acne cannot be cured and it is therefore important to use a skin care routine that can be used for an extended period without any negative side effects. The problem with many anti-acne drugs such as Roaccutan is that they tear on the body without providing long-lasting effect on the problem skin. In order for these drugs to work, they are then required to be used against teenage acne where the problem can disappear with age. As adult acne is more prolonged, it is especially important to use a method that takes care of the skin.

Therefore, we use a method that helps you to restore the skin's natural balance and then maintain that balance to keep the problem skin under control. It starts with a free skin consultation where we look at your skin to find out what kind of problem skin you have and what skin care products are right for your skin type.

Step 2 is that we perform a recommended treatment which in this case is the acne treatment which gives you a really good start on the treatment of your skin. For acne skin, we have a dedicated acne treatment that cleanses the skin in depth, clears out the pores and kills the bacteria that cause the acne.

The next step is to take care of your skin yourself with the help of DAHL Skincare's skin care products. These skin care products restore the skin's natural balance and then maintain this balance to keep the acne under control. Click on step 1 to get started!
